Qutr Phrase Structure Tagset includes the following:

SENT - complete, unmodifiable sentences
VP - templates and verb phrases containing * (all tenses and modes)
NP - nouns and noun phrases (common and proper)
ADJ - adjectives
ADV - adverbs
NUM - cardinal numbers
PRT - particles or other function words

sent -> sent
np vp -> sent
adj np -> np
num -> adj
num prt -> np (eg 5 + day -> 5 days)
np vp adv -> sent
adj -> sent
adv -> sent
